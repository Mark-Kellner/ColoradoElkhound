Why did we change the appearance of ColoradoElkhound.Com?

We did this by making the following changes:

 
  1. We changed the interface to a menu driven site to give you quick easy access to our whole website from the homepage.

  2. We wanted to provide more information than before without adding another layer of complexity to the site. Our hope is we accomplished this and you'll find our website to be a good resource to learn about Norwegian Elkhounds.

  3. The old site was built several years ago and has became very difficult to maintain, much less to introduce new material to it without impacting our operational site.  In the future new material will appear on our site with minimal impact to the operation of the working site (or the site that is in operation).
 

Our sincere hope is the changes that you see will be a benefit to you and that our website will be inviting and easier to use. After all the World Wide Web should be a positive experience and not a hassle.  We changed the interface to a menu driven site to give you quick easy access to our whole website from the homepage.

We welcome any constructive criticism regarding our website at: webmaster@coloradoelkhound.com
